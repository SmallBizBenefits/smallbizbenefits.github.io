What Small Business Owners Actually Need to Know About Benefits

Most of what small business owners believe about health insurance is wrong — or outdated. The group insurance market that excluded businesses under 10 employees is no longer the only option. Individual marketplace plans, employer contribution models, and no-minimum-enrollment packages have changed what's possible for businesses of any size.

This site exists to explain those options without the sales pitch.
